Scanning networks without authorization is illegal and unethical. Always obtain written permission.

Host Discovery

Use ping scan to discover live hosts:

nmap -sn 10.10.10.0/24

Fast Scans

Top ports help when time is constrained:

nmap --top-ports 1000 10.10.10.10

Version & Default Scripts

nmap -sV -sC 10.10.10.10
Tip: Combine with -oN to save results.
